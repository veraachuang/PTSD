Find Help

Remember, you don't have to go through it alone. There are caring professionals and resources available to support you on your path to healing.

Crisis Hotlines

If you are thinking about harming yourself or others, please call one of the hotlines below

  • Crisis Connection: Call (206)-461-3222
  • 24 Hour Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call 988
  • Trans Lifeline: Call (877)-565-8860

Mental Health Service Providers

For non-crisis help, take the time visit one of the links below

  • Seattle Mental Health Clinic
  • King County Mental Health Servicese
  • Zodoc: Book Local Doctors and Professionals

Addiction Services

If you or a loved one are suffering from addiction, time is of the essence. Reach out now

  • SAMHSA National Helpline: Call 1-(800)-662-4357
  • Hazelden Betty Ford Foundation
  • Washington Recovery Helpline: Call 1-(866)-789-1511
